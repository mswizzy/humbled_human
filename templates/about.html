About


The Humbled Human project is designed to allow any and all interested parties to discover, learn, and share about organizations that are pushing for a positive change for basic human rights. Over the past several years there has been an increase in attention and growth of movements and organizations contributing to the obtainment of these rights.

With the creation of this project, users will be able to create Contributor accounts in which they can indicate specific human rights movements they are actively involved with and share links/information about them. For the public, they will be able to view content added by Contributors and can create accounts to become Contributors.
